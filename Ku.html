Kutsinta Mindanao

Description

Kutsinta Mindanao A brown rice cake, the kutsinta can go hand in hand with puto or they can be eaten all on their own (with freshly shaved coconut). They are also made from rice flour.

History

Kutsinta Mindanao is a type of steamed rice cake originated in mindanao. it is made from a mixture of rice flour, brown sugar and lye, enchanced with yellow food coloring or annatto extract, and streamed in small ramekins. it is coated with coconut meat

Suggested Retail Price

30pcs of kutsinta is Php150

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3cups water
  • 1 1/2 tsp lye water
  • 2 tsp anatto seeds

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, mix together flour and sugar.
  • Slowly pour water, mix well to avoid lumps. Add lye water, achuete water and vanilla. Mix.
  • Pour in molds. Steam for 10 minutes. Serve with grated fresh coconut.
